Welcome to the AGGORA Technical timesheet management app – a handy way to manage your timesheet on the go!


The Employee Timesheet app is an alternative to sign in sheets and was developed with both employees and human resources professionals in mind. It is a daily timesheet that helps both parties keep track of time and attendance. It records all types of employee hours including Jobs, travel, lunch and other misc tasks. This app aims to minimize the time spent by engineers for doing their timesheets. Employees are expected to checkin at the beginnning of each day and checkout at the end of each working day. Timesheet automatically retreives all the job and travel related information with a simple push of button. It is ever so easy to add the time spent on other activities during the working hours such as lunch, parts collection, etc by simply dragging the time slots on the calendar. It's easy to record and send timesheets so engineers are less likely to forget to enter billable hours for jobs that are away from the office. With a few clicks the timesheets will be sent back to the office leading to faster billing.
